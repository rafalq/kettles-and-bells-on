We make people fit & strong

I can walk again!

After years of chronic back pain that left me hunched over and reliant on a cane, the Kettlebell Senior Program changed everything. The gentle yet progressive swings, Turkish get-ups, and mobility drills rebuilt my strength, improved my posture, and restored my confidence. Now, at 68, I stroll through the park with my grandkids without a single wince—proof that it's never too late to reclaim your body.

Winner of every pub fight!

After decades of scrapping in dimly lit pubs and waking up with bruised knuckles and a sore ego, the Warrior Kettlebell program turned me into an unbreakable force. The explosive snatches, crushing presses, and battle-tested flows forged raw power in my core, sharpened my reflexes, and taught me control without chaos. I walk into any bar with quiet confidence—no fights needed, because the real victory is the beast I've become.
