Certification Preparation

SFG I

  • 4 week intensive course
  • Max 10 participants
  • 2 classes per week
  • Guided by SFG-certified instructors
  • Difficulty: all levels (with ambition!)

This intensive 4-week program is specifically designed to prepare you for the world-renowned StrongFirst SFG Level I Kettlebell Instructor Certification.

You will master the six fundamental movements (Swing, Get-up, Clean, Press, Squat, Snatch) to the exact StrongFirst technical standards, build the necessary strength-endurance, and learn how to teach these movements safely and effectively to others.

Whether your goal is to pass the SFG I certification on your first attempt or simply take your personal kettlebell practice to an elite level – this course is your fastest, most reliable path to success.

SFG II

  • 6 week advanced course
  • Max 8 participants
  • 3 classes per week
  • Guided by SFG II-certified instructors
  • Difficulty: SFG I required + serious ambition

This rigorous 6-week program is designed for certified SFG Level I instructors and serious athletes ready to pursue the prestigious StrongFirst SFG Level II Certification.

You will refine your technique to competition-grade precision, master advanced variations and progressions, develop elite-level strength-endurance, and deepen your coaching ability through hands-on practice and detailed movement analysis.

This course demands dedication, discipline, and a genuine commitment to the StrongFirst methodology. You'll train alongside like-minded professionals under the guidance of SFG II-certified instructors who will push you beyond your current limits and prepare you for one of the most demanding certifications in the strength world.

If you're ready to join the ranks of elite kettlebell instructors and take your practice to the absolute highest level – this is your next step. Official SFG I requirements & standards:

RKC

  • 3-day intensive workshop (Fri–Sun)
  • Max 20–24 participants
  • Full-day training + testing on Sunday
  • Guided by RKC-certified instructors
  • Difficulty: intermediate–advanced (prior kettlebell experience required)

This focused 3-day RKC (Russian Kettlebell Challenge) workshop is designed for intermediate to advanced trainees who want to test their skills, earn their RKC certification, or simply experience the legendary intensity of old-school kettlebell training.

Over Friday, Saturday, and Sunday, you'll drill the core RKC movements with uncompromising attention to technique, build serious work capacity, and push your mental and physical limits in a supportive yet demanding environment.

Sunday concludes with an official testing session where you'll demonstrate your mastery of the fundamentals under pressure. Whether you're pursuing RKC certification or simply want to prove what you're capable of, this workshop will challenge you like nothing else.

Expect high volume, high intensity, and high standards. Come prepared to work hard, learn fast, and leave stronger than you arrived. → dragondoor.com/rkc-certification

Specialized Programs

Senior Program

  • 8 week course
  • Up to 6 participants
  • 2 gentle classes per week
  • Guided by SFG certified Instructors with senior expertise
  • Difficulty: beginner, adapted for 55+

This thoughtfully designed 8-week program brings the proven benefits of kettlebell training to adults 55 and older in a safe, supportive, and age-appropriate environment.

You'll build functional strength for daily activities, improve balance and mobility, increase bone density, and enhance overall quality of life—all through carefully adapted kettlebell movements that respect your body's needs and capabilities.

Our SFG-certified instructors have specialized experience working with senior athletes and understand how to progress each individual at the right pace. Classes are small (max 6 participants), allowing for personalized attention and modifications as needed.

Whether you're completely new to strength training or looking to maintain your vitality and independence as you age, this program will help you move better, feel stronger, and stay active for years to come.

Women Program

  • Up to 10 participants
  • 2 classes per week
  • Guided by female SFG-certified instructors

Whether your goal is fat loss, athletic performance, postpartum recovery, or simply feeling powerful and capable in your own skin—this program will transform not just how you look, but how you move through the world. Strong women aren't born. They're built. One rep at a time.

Warrior Kettlebell

  • 8 week combat-strength fusion
  • Up to 8 fighters
  • 3 sessions: 2 KB + 1 striking
  • Guided by SFG & certified boxing coach
  • Difficulty: beginner to intermediate

This unique 8-week program combines the explosive power of kettlebell training with striking fundamentals to create combat-ready strength, speed, and conditioning.

You'll develop the kind of functional power that translates directly to the ring or cage: grip strength for clinching, rotational power for hooks and kicks, bulletproof core stability, and the cardiovascular capacity to maintain intensity through multiple rounds.

With 2 kettlebell sessions and 1 striking-focused session per week, you'll train under the guidance of both an SFG-certified instructor and a certified boxing coach who understand how strength work and combat sports reinforce each other.

This isn't bodybuilding. This is building a fighter's body—strong, explosive, and relentless. Whether you're an active competitor or a martial artist looking to add serious horsepower to your game, this program will make you a more dangerous athlete.
