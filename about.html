About Us

Building strength, character, and community through the ancient art of kettlebell training

ü™∂ Our Story

From Passion to Purpose

Kettles & Bells On was founded in 2018 by Pavel Tsatsouline disciples who recognized the transformative power of kettlebell training. What started as a small garage gym has grown into a thriving community of strength seekers, from young athletes to seniors reclaiming their vitality.

Our facility emerged from a simple belief: real strength isn't built in isolation, but through shared struggle and mutual support. We've created a space where every swing, every press, and every get-up is part of a larger journey toward becoming stronger versions of ourselves.

Excellence

Accessibility
